Our Mission

To give every person and organisation a single, verifiable digital identity that puts them in charge of every notification, message, and connection they send or receive — removing noise, spam, and unwanted outreach for good.

🔭

Our Vision

A world where your digital presence is truly yours. Where discovering a brand, attending an event, or joining a community is a seamless, privacy-first experience — and every sender is accountable for what lands in your inbox.

The problem we solve

Notifications are broken. Your email is flooded, your phone never stops buzzing, and half of it is from brands or communities you barely remember signing up for. Whodini fixes that with a consent-first, identity-driven layer on top of every channel you already use.

How Whodini works

Three simple steps stand between you and a cleaner digital life.

01

Create your identity

Sign up and choose an account type that matches who you are — personal, business, community, event, or agency.

02

Set your preferences

Decide exactly what you want to hear about, from whom, and on which channel. You stay in the driver's seat at all times.

Built for every kind of identity

Whodini isn't one-size-fits-all. Five distinct account experiences, each crafted for a different way of connecting.

Personal

Your private profile. Control which brands, communities, and events can reach you. Curate your notification feed so only what matters gets through — nothing else.

  • Granular notification preferences
  • Private subscription management
  • Unified inbox across channels

Business

Send targeted promotional alerts exclusively to people who have opted in. Reach a genuinely engaged audience and measure real impact, not just open rates.

  • Subscriber-only broadcasts
  • Engagement analytics dashboard
  • Multi-channel campaign delivery

Community

Keep every member in the loop. From neighbourhood groups to online fan bases, Whodini gives community leaders the tools to communicate clearly and respectfully.

  • Member management & roles
  • Announcement broadcasts
  • Discussion thread notifications

Event

Organise attendees, vendors, and sponsors in one place. Send reminders, schedule updates, and post-event follow-ups without anyone falling through the cracks.

  • Attendee & vendor coordination
  • Automated reminders & alerts
  • Real-time schedule updates

Agency

Manage multiple client campaigns under a single roof. Switch contexts instantly, keep client data siloed, and deliver consistent results across every brand you represent.

  • Multi-client workspace
  • Unified campaign calendar
  • White-label reporting
