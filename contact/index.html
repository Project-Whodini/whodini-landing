Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to the things people ask most.

What is Whodini?
Whodini is a digital identity platform that lets individuals, businesses, communities, events, and agencies take control of how they communicate and connect. You decide what you receive, from whom, and through which channel.
Is Whodini free to use?
Personal accounts are free. Business, Community, Event, and Agency accounts have plans designed to match their scale — reach out to us or sign up to explore the options.
How long does it take to hear back from support?
We aim to respond to all enquiries within one business day. For urgent technical issues, please include "URGENT" in your message subject so our team can prioritise accordingly.
How does Whodini protect my privacy?
Privacy is at the core of everything we build. Your data is never sold to third parties. You have full control over who can contact you and you can revoke access at any time.
Can I switch account types later?
Yes — you can upgrade or change your account type at any time from your profile settings. Your existing connections and preferences are preserved during the transition.
